About Kopi Senja

Born from the stillness of early evenings, Kopi Senja is a quiet pause in the middle of the city.

We believe coffee should be both grounding and gentle. Our space is designed for unhurried moments—soft light pooling on wooden tables, the low murmur of conversation, the comforting rhythm of beans being ground to order.

Every cup we serve is roasted in small batches, brewed with care, and paired with an ambience that invites you to stay a little longer than you planned.
