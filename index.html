About

A refuge in the heart of the city

Jogja Coffee House was conceived for those who value intention over haste. Located in Yogyakarta, where tradition and creativity converge, we offer a setting that honours both the craft of coffee and the need for stillness.

Our space is designed for professionals, creatives, and travellers who seek a place to think, meet, or simply pause. Every detail—from the selection of beans to the acoustics of the room—is considered to support focus and calm.

Philosophy

We believe that the best work, the clearest ideas, and the most meaningful conversations often arrive when the world outside slows down. Here, we do not rush the brew or the moment. We hold space for what emerges when you allow it.

A place where the rhythm of Jogja by night meets the discipline of craft.
